About company

At BORKY S.G. Bakre & Associates, we are dedicated to driving positive change in the world through environmental stewardship and sustainable solutions. Our commitment to a greener, more sustainable future is at the heart of everything we do. Our Mission:
Our mission is simple yet profound: to create a world where humanity and the environment coexist harmoniously. We believe that responsible environmental practices are not just a choice but a responsibility, and we are here to help individuals, businesses, and organizations embrace this responsibility.
We, at BORKY S. G. BAKRE & ASSOCIATES, are a group of Industrial Designers, Engineers, Technologists, and Economists. We are a Government Accredited Environmental and Energy Consulting and Contracting Firm; with many years of experience and success in Planning and Developing Industrial Equipment, Techniques, and Solutions - helping different industries to Transform Technology into Efficiently Operating Plants and Facilities.
