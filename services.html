Our Services
Environmental Safety and Public Health
ERP/SCCP
Emergency Response Plan/Spill Control and Countermeasure Plan – in line with regulatory requirements, we work with clients to develop ERP/SCCP tailored to their specific operational requirements.
PER
Preliminary Evaluation Report – we carry out PER for projects whose impact on the environment is of less consequence to require an EIA in accordance to environmental laws.
Fire Safety Plan
Fire Safety Plan – we design the Fire Safety Plan with the cooperation of the facility owner to identify the actions that should be taken by the occupants and management in the event of a fire or similar emergency situation. The Fire Safety Plan covers fire prevention, evacuation and emergency response.
EIA
Environmental Impact Assessment – we carry out EIA study from the start and also review existing EIA to help improve the accuracy of the reports; and also help in securing EIA certificates in accordance with the EIA Act 1992.
EER
Environmental Evaluation Report – We work with operators or licensees, whose operations have been observed to have significant and adverse environmental effects and impacts, to prepare EER in line regulatory requirements.
PIA
Post Impact Assessment – In furtherance of our EIA services, we work with clients to carry out PIA studies on projects whose implementation has commenced following the completion of the EIA process.
EAR
Environmental Audit Report – We prepare EAR and carry out EA on existing facilities, on behalf of clients, to objectively study of how well the facilities are performing (in terms of operations on the environment and overall environment managemental actions) in comparison to set Environmental Regulatory Standards.
EMP
Environmental Management Plan – To ensure that undue or reasonably avoidable adverse impacts of their activities are prevented and that the positive benefits of their operations are enhanced, we work with clients to prepare EMPs, which will usually be site specific.
WMP
Waste Management Plan – In line with environmental laws, every facility likely to generate wastes of one form or the other should have a WMP prepared. We, for this purpose, work with clients to prepare WMPs that will be specific to their needs towards wastes reduction, in conformance to Government set standards.
EMR
Environmental Management Review - As required by law, that facilities with significant environmental impacts, should annually prepare EMR (“a review of an individual facility’s programme and management systems to determine the extent to which a facility has developed and implemented specific environmental protection programmes and plans which, if properly managed, should ensure compliance and progress towards environmental excellence”) for submission, we serve clients in carrying out this exercise.
Safety Case
Since it is mandatory by law that the operator prepares for an offshore facility, safety case; we work with such operators to the safety case, which will identify the safety critical aspects of the facility, both technical and managerial, with respect to major accident events, describes how the risks are controlled, and the safety management system in place to ensure the controls are effectively and consistently applied.
EMS
Environmental Management System Development – In this service, we assist the client in developing a framework for assessing and managing environmental and sustainability impacts. This will provide a commitment to achieving improved sustainability performance and legal compliance and will help to embed sustainability within the Company’s operations.
Plant and Process Study/Improvement
We carry out this service on the clients’ entire plants, processes or business, to determine how they can be operated more efficiently thereby reducing costs and maximizing profits.
Waste Reduction Feasibility Studies
We, here, carry out, on behalf of clients, waste audits, study waste reduction options and feasibility methods of reducing, reusing, recycling and recovering wastes.
Waste Evacuation and Cleaning Services
We evacuate, on behalf of clients and in collaboration with relevant governmental regulatory agencies, special process wastes; and also undertake cleanup and advisory services.
Pollution Abatement/Control Devices and Equipment
We design, construct/install, operate/manage and maintain these plants to meet the specific needs of various clients and waste streams. Besides ensuring that the equipment treat wastes to meet or even better set limits contained environmental laws, we undertake performance evaluation of already installed (third party) facilities and then improve wherever necessary.
Erosion Control, Land Reclamation and Wetland Resources Management
The services here are site specific, and based on prevailing regulations.
Noise Audits
We undertake these services either directly on behalf of clients, for monitoring purposes, or on behalf of third parties in cases where noise pollution disputes exist. In whichever case, we measure the noise levels, compare results with set limits, diagnose causes where problems exist, and then use the information to determine how to efficiently reduce and control noise pollution.
Water and Waste Water Audits
These audits are conducted prior to design and sizing of Effluent Treatment Plants (ETPs). Through the Water and Waste Water Audit, the operator will be able to accurately know the actual quantity of water being consumed for various purposes and, through water conservation measures, reduce the amount of water being used and hence the quantity of wastewater being discharged. Consequently, the ETP size can be more appropriately determined.
Gardens and Parks
We undertake design and development of gardens and parks with health and social factors in mind.
HSE Consulting
Our services here include: Advising the clients on policy matters related to Health, Safety and Environmental Legislation; Developing a Total Waste Management Philosophy to ensure maximum efficiency of Anti-pollution Facility Provisions within the Facility with the co-operation of the client; Supervising permit taking and mandatory monitoring and also reporting to the relevant control agencies; Deploying measuring instruments to provide information; Submission of reports on the state of Health, Safety and Environment in respect of the facility.
Traffic Assessment
We undertake comprehensive and systematic studies to identify the impact of the development in ‘person trips’, which are then broken down by transport mode, relating to a proposed development.
Technical Risks Assessment
We, on behalf of clients, carry out technical risk assessments of their projects and operations generally.
Composting
We offer technical support services in ensuring the effectiveness of the composting process, which is employed to recycle organic matter present in agricultural and also municipal solid wastes back into the soil to maintain soil fertility.
